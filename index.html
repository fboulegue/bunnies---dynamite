Welcome to bunnies and dynamite

you can help heroes

Bunnies and Dynamite is a Charity Calendar Project to raise funds for Help for Heroes. We are a group of german/english women, based in Paderborn, Germany, planning a pinup project to aid the Help for Heroes cause. Most of us have a partner who is a member of the British Army. The aim is to produce a calendar with pin-up pictures. We are aware of the fact that this idea isn t brand new, but in this part of europe it hasn t been done before.

100% of the profit of the calendar goes straight to the Help for Heroes charity, all that is deducted is the printing cost of the calendar itself and postage. Everyone on the Bunnies and Dynamite team does this in their own free time for a cause that is close to their hearts and as such, every cent we make goes straight to the charity, we are strictly a non profit project.
